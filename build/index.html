About Us

Abdulrahman Saleh Al Rajhi & Partners Co. Ltd. is a leading company specializing in general trade and investment. Founded with the mission to drive economic growth and foster innovative business solutions, we are committed to delivering excellence and creating value for our clients and partners.

Our Vision

The group's investment principles center around taking a long-term, proactive, and value investment view to its portfolio.

Our Mission

We invest selectively and limit the number of companies we participate in to ensure we can continually add value through our own internal resources. We bring to the table extensive strategic, operational, and financial expertise and strive to work closely with the management of the investee companies in order to realize their long-term potential.
